13.2 If the LESSEE should desire electrical current beyond that is available at the PREMISES at

the time of the execution of this lease, the LESSEE shall pay the cost of installing

equipment required for that purpose, whereby the LESSEE shall first notify the LESSOR

and receive its written approval before such equipment shall be installed.

13.3 The LESSEE shall not do or permit any act or allow any occupier of the PREMISES not

allowed under the lease agreement in the conduct of its business or otherwise on the

PREMISES which shall be or may become a nuisance.

written consent of the LESSOR first being obtained, which consent shall not be

to any compensation thereto. A list of alterations, additions or improvements which the

LESSEE might contemplate or propose during the currency of this lease, shall be submitted

in writing to the LESSOR for the LESSOR’S approval, which shall be a prerequisite to the

LESSEE making any such alterations, additions or improvements.

13.5 At the termination of this lease, whether by affluxion of time or otherwise, the LESSOR shall,

at its own option, be entitled to call upon the LESSEE to restore the PREMISES to the same

condition as it were before the alteration or additions were effected, or to accept the

PREMISES with such alterations or additions, in which event the LESSOR shall not be

obliged to compensate the LESSEE in respect thereof.

13.6 Notwithstanding anything to the contrary herein contained, the LESSEE shall have the right,

simultaneously with the expiration of the lease, to remove any fixtures and/or fittings and/or

signs and/or partitions installed by it in or upon the PREMISES, provided that it shall make

good any damage to the PREMISES caused as a result of the removal of such fixtures

and/or fittings and/or signs and/or partitions. All fitted carpets and/or installed flooring are to

remain the property of the LESSOR.

13.7 The LESSEE shall not be entitled to affix any signs, written or otherwise, anywhere on or

about the PREMISES hereby let without the permission and approval of the LESSOR or

the LESSOR’S architects first being obtained, and subject to such conditions as may be

imposed by either of them respectively. All signs shall, at the termination of this lease,

whether by affluxion of time or otherwise, be removed by the LESSEE at its own expense,

failing which the LESSOR shall be entitled to do so and recover the costs thereof from the

LESSEE.

13.8 The LESSEE shall keep the PREMISES clean and in a tidy condition save for waste

produced in the ordinary cause of production and/or the LESSEE’s trade activities.

13.9 The LESSEE shall implement an environmentally conscious waste management system

with regard to the disposal and/or recycling of its waste from the LESSEE’s business

13.10 The LESSEE shall not contravene or permit any contravention of any law, regulation,

all times conform to the same.

15. DESTRUCTION OF PREMISES

Should the building PREMISES be destroyed or substantially damaged by fire or by any

other cause, or should the PREMISES be so destroyed or damaged to an extent which

prevents the LESSEE from having beneficial occupation of the PREMISES, then:-

15.1.1 The LESSEE shall have no claim of any nature whatsoever against the LESSOR

as a result thereof;

15.1.2 The LESSOR shall be entitled to determine whether or not this lease shall be

cancelled and shall notify the LESSEE of its decision in writing; and

15.1.3 Should the LESSOR not notify the LESSEE of the decision within 1 (ONE) month

after such destruction or damage, then it shall be deemed that the LESSOR has

elected to cancel this lease.

15.2 Should the LESSOR elect or be deemed to have elected to cancel this lease then the

LESSEE shall have no claim of any nature whatsoever against the LESSOR as a

result of the cancellation of the Lease.

15.3 Should the LESSOR elect not to cancel this lease then:-

15.3.1 The LESSOR shall re-instate the PREMISES at its own cost as expeditiously as

is reasonably possible in the circumstances, making any reasonable alteration in

the design and structure thereof as may be necessary or desirable;

15.3.2 The LESSEE shall not be liable for any rental for so long as it is deprived of beneficial

occupation of the PREMISES; and

15.3.3 Should the LESSEE take beneficial occupation from time to time of any part of the

PREMISES, then the LESSEE shall pay rental thereof on a pro- rata basis, as may be

determined by the LESSOR.

15.4 Should any part, but not the whole of the PREMISES, be destroyed or damaged by fire or

any other cause then:-

15.4.1 This lease shall not be cancelled;

15.4.2 The rental then payable by the LESSEE shall be reduced pro-rata having regard to the

extent to which, and the period for which, it is deprived of beneficial occupation of the

PREMISES; and

15.4.3 The LESSOR shall repair at its own cost damaged or destroyed portions of the

PREMISES as expeditiously as is reasonably possible in the circumstances.

15.5 Any dispute between the LESSOR and the LESSEE in regard to the amount of remission of

rental and the date on which, or period for which the PREMISES are available or

unavailable for occupation by the LESSEE shall be decided either by the LESSOR’S

attorneys and/or auditors/accounting officers.
